Ingredients

  • A pot
  • A instant ramen pack
  • A bowl
  • Water

Directions

  1. Put the water in the pot.
  2. Heat the water until it boils.
  3. Remove the noodles from the package and put them into the boiling water.
  4. Open the seasoning packet and throw the powder with the noodles.
  5. Cook for approximately 3 minutes.
  6. Drain off the excess water but leave some to make a little broth.
  7. Pour the contents of the pot into the bowl.

And it's finished! Now you have an "original" ramen to enjoy!
