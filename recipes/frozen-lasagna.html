Frozen Lasagna

Ingredients

  • A frozen lasagna pack
  • A microwave oven

Directions

  1. Remove all the packaging (it can be either cardboard or plastic).
  2. Carefully remove the plastic sheet covering the lasagna.
  3. Put the lasagna in the microwave oven and select the apropriate option to start cooking.
  4. If your oven, for any reason, doesn't have a dedicated "Cook Lasagna" option, microwave it for approximately 20 minutes in high potency.
  • Attention: If you see smoke coming out of the oven, you overcooked the lasagna and now it is inedible. Get another one and try again.

If everything went smoothly, you now should have a "delicious" lasagna. Enjoy!
